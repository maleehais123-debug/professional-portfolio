Entity Campaign Planning & Execution

An end-to-end breakdown of a strategic e-commerce campaign, from initial problem identification to final metrics and key learnings.

1. Campaign Overview

Entity Campaign

My Role: Marketing Executive & CRO, responsible for campaign execution, on-site optimisation and performance improvements

Objective: To increase brand awareness and trust for our OEM gaming line, Entity, by improving its visibility, strengthening on-site presence and creating a more cohesive user journey that encourages engagement and conversion

2. Goals & KPIs

  • Increase Traffic
  • Boost Conversions
  • Improve CTR

3. Audience

Targeting specific segments such as budget-conscious buyers vs performance gamers.

4. The Problem

The Entity gaming range lacked brand awareness, meaning customers were less likely to trust or choose it over more recognisable competitors. This resulted in low engagement and reduced conversion, despite the products being competitively positioned.

The campaign aimed to increase visibility and build trust by introducing a stronger brand presence across the site. This involved improving how the Entity range was presented through clearer messaging, more prominent placement and a more cohesive user journey, making it easier for users to discover, understand and confidently purchase from the brand.

5. What I Did

  • End-to-End Campaign Delivery: I planned and executed the launch of Entity as a new OEM gaming brand across onsite and offsite channels ensuring consistent messaging and a premium brand position.
  • High-Visibility Onsite Strategy: I managed key placements including homepage hot deal tiles carousel banners and promotional page features to maximise visibility and drive user engagement.
  • Conversion-Focused UX & Layout: I refined landing page structure improved content hierarchy and strengthened calls-to-action to support a clearer path to purchase.
  • Value-Led Messaging: I implemented consistent incentive messaging including free delivery and a £10 voucher across all touchpoints to reduce hesitation and increase conversion intent.
  • Email Social & Affiliate Activation: I supported the campaign through dedicated email campaigns paid social activity and affiliate partnerships to expand reach and introduce Entity to new audiences.
  • Brand Trust & Positioning: I ensured Entity was presented alongside established gaming brands using high-quality visuals technical specifications and consistent design to build credibility and customer confidence.

6. Channels Used

Website Email Facebook

7. Key Results

+18%

CTR

+12%

Sales

+22%

Engagement

8. Key Learnings

What Worked

High-visibility placements such as the homepage hot deal significantly outperformed lower priority positions reinforcing the impact of prominent positioning on user engagement and interaction. Increased exposure directly contributed to stronger click performance and brand awareness.

Consistent messaging across multiple channels including onsite placements email paid social and affiliates helped reinforce the Entity proposition and create a more cohesive user experience across touchpoints.

Value-led incentives such as free delivery and a £10 voucher proved effective in capturing attention and encouraging engagement supporting both click performance and conversion.

Affiliate activity and positioning alongside trusted third-party platforms helped strengthen perceived credibility which is critical when introducing an OEM brand into a competitive market.

What I'd Improve

Brand awareness takes time to translate into conversion so I would extend campaign duration and introduce more sustained CRM activity to nurture users and build stronger familiarity with the Entity range.

I would further optimise CTR across channels particularly within paid social and email by testing variations in messaging creative and subject lines to better understand what resonates most with users.

I would also expand the use of high-performing placements such as homepage hot deals across future campaigns using data to prioritise visibility and maximise engagement.

Increasing consistency in branding and messaging across all platforms would further strengthen recognition and trust supporting long-term growth for the Entity brand.
